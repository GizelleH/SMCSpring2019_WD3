Our Team

The team the makes Flutter happen everyday.

  • Hannah L. To

    Hannah realized that she could merge her love for science and nature at Flutter. Hannah is currently a BioSCAN and UNRC research intern where she studies local bee diversity and how urban environments are affecting butterfly populations. She has worked to familiarize herself with the over 200 butterfly species that are native to California. She spends her time catching butterflies in Los Angeles gardens or making sure that butterfly specimens are looking beautiful in the lab. Hannah hopes to show the world how exciting and beautiful the butterflies in our city can be!
