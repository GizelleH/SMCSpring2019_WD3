Education Programs

Assisting Title 1 and low-income students in meeting and enhancing their science curriculum, Flutter is able to host thousands of children via school tours.

Workshops

Flutter’s has unique and educational workshops, like How to Build a Butterfly Garden, Yoga in the Rainforest, and Waking up with the Butterflies as well as its outreach program to schools, contribute to the efforts of the Foundation as well.

View details »

Wings of Remembrance

Donations and purchases made through our Wings of Remembrance program, a memorial that honors the loss of loved ones via beautiful and colorful butterflies hung in Flutter’s main foyer, also supports the causes of the Foundation.
